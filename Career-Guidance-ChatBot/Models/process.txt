Download & Configure the Model


Download the model: mistral-7b-instruct-v0.2.Q4_K_M.gguf or mistral-7b-instruct-v0.1.Q4_0.gguf 
or tinyllama-1.1b-chat-v1.0.Q4_0.gguf from https://huggingface.co/ and Locate your model file 
and update the model path in chatbot.py with correct path 
like : model_path = "C:/path/to/mistral-7b-instruct-v0.2.Q4_K_M.gguf"  Save the file after making changes.

[ you can use another model depend on your purpose ]
